##############
Controls
##############

You have two ways of controlling the 3D nebula set-up:

#. Use the :ref:`control panel` that manages all the nodes and particle systems:

    .. image:: ../_static/control_panel_preview.jpg
        :alt: Nebula Generator
        :width: 100%
        :target: control_panel.html

#. Use the shader :ref:`Nodes<Nodes and Particles>` directly in the files to control the shape of the nebula, and the Nebula Box Object's :ref:`Particle System settings<Nodes and Particles>` settings to control the stars and lighting:

    .. image:: ../_static/3d_version_nodes.png
        :alt: Nebula Generator
        :width: 100%
        :target: nodes_particles.html
